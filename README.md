# PRIVACYPOLICYRAWDOGGING
Privacy Policy Rawdogging
Effective date: [01-03-2026]

Rawdogging respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

Information We Collect

Rawdogging does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

The app:

• Does not require an account
• Does not collect personal data
• Does not use analytics
• Does not track your activity outside the app
• Does not share data with third parties

All session data and statistics are stored locally on your device and never leave your phone.

Purchases

Rawdogging offers a 5 day free trial followed by a one time payment to unlock lifetime access.

All payments are processed securely by Apple through your Apple ID. Rawdogging does not have access to your payment information, credit card details, or billing data.

For more information about how Apple handles payment data, please refer to Apple’s Privacy Policy.

App Permissions

Rawdogging may require system permissions in order to block access to apps during active sessions. These permissions are used solely for the core functionality of the app.

Rawdogging does not access the content of your apps, messages, calls, photos, or any personal data.

The app only enforces the restrictions you choose during a session.

Data Storage

All data generated by Rawdogging, including session history and statistics, is stored locally on your device.

If you delete the app, all associated data is permanently removed.

Children’s Privacy

Rawdogging does not knowingly collect or process any data from children. Since the app does not collect personal information, it is safe to use without providing personal data.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any updates will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.
